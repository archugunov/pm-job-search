# Outreach Templates

<!--
  Generic outreach starting points. Skills load this file when drafting
  warm/cold messages so you can stay consistent. Edit any template; add new
  categories as you discover patterns. Keep entries short — three sentences
  is the ceiling for most channels.
-->

## Connection note — LinkedIn, 2nd-degree (warm)

> Hi {{first_name}} — saw you're at {{company}}. {{specific_observation}}.
> I'm exploring {{target_role_category}} roles in {{industry}}; would value
> 15 min to hear how the team's thinking. Open to it?

## After applying — LinkedIn, hiring manager

> Hi {{first_name}} — I applied for the {{role}} role yesterday and wanted
> to put a face on the application. {{one_specific_thing_about_the_company}}.
> Happy to share more on what I'd focus on in the first 90 days if useful.

## Speculative — recruiter / talent partner

> Hi {{first_name}} — {{shared_context_or_referral}}. I'm looking at
> {{target_role_category}} roles in {{industry}}, {{geography_constraint}}.
> Open to a quick chat if any of your current briefs might fit.

## Follow-up — post-interview thank-you

> Thanks for the time today, {{first_name}}. {{one_specific_thing_from_the_conversation}}.
> Happy to dig into {{topic_we_skimmed}} ahead of the next step if useful.

## Re-engagement — Rejected/Closed company, new role

> Hi {{first_name}} — we spoke about {{previous_role}} in {{month}}. Saw
> {{new_role}} just opened — wanted to flag interest. Worth a short call?
