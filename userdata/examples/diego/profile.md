## Positioning

I'm a Head of Product with twelve years across developer tools and B2B SaaS, most recently as VP Product at a Series C workflow-automation company doing roughly $40M ARR with a self-serve top of funnel and a usage-based pricing model. Before that I was Group PM for the platform surface at a public DevTools company, owning the integrations and API products for three years.

The work I'm best at is taking a PLG B2B product from "growth working, not sure why" to "growth working, instrumented, defensible." That means building the analytics and pricing surface, hiring 2-3 senior ICs, and getting the leadership team comfortable with the difference between feature requests and the things that actually move retention. I write specs in public, I do my own SQL, and I'm comfortable being the most senior product voice in a small leadership team.

Open to Head of Product or VP Product at a Series B/C B2B SaaS or DevTools company. Fully remote with North/South Americas overlap. Open to advisory engagements alongside the main role.

- **Cross-functional spec process.** Introduced a public spec-and-review process across product/eng/design — now the company's onboarding artefact for every new PM hire.
