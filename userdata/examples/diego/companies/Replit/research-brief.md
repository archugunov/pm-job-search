## Company snapshot

Replit is a browser-based IDE combined with an AI-native code-generation layer and an education arm serving K-12 and university learners. ~150 ppl, Series B. The Head of Product, Education role owns the K-12 and university surface — curriculum partnerships, classroom tooling, and student acquisition.

## Why this fits

Wouldn't take it. Two reasons, both clean.

First: the Education segment is a specific positioning bet I don't have proof points for. K-12 product, classroom integrations, and institution partnerships are a different craft from the B2B SaaS + dev-tools lane I'm building on. I'd be starting from scratch on domain knowledge that matters a lot here.

Second: Replit's core AI-native code-gen play is far from my dev-tools-PLG framing. My positioning is apps + workflow tooling for technical operators — not AI-assisted coding or consumer-education plays.

Capturing this as a deliberate no: it's not that Replit is a bad company, it's that this role would require me to pivot my positioning rather than extend it. Not pursuing AI-codegen plays this search.
