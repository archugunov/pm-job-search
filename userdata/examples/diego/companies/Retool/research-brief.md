## Company snapshot

Retool is a visual builder for internal tools at engineering orgs. Drag-and-drop components connect to databases, APIs, and third-party services. Self-serve free tier converts to team and enterprise paid plans — the PLG motion is the primary growth engine. ~280 ppl total; the Apps team I'd be owning is ~30. Series C-equivalent. Recent moves: they launched a workflow automation layer adjacent to the apps surface — this is notable because it extends the ICP beyond pure app-builders into workflow-heavy ops teams.

Apps team scope at ~30 ppl puts me close to the founder without being too early-stage. Compensation: HoP at Retool likely lands $230-260K USD per Levels.fyi data — above my stated $190-230K band, which I'm comfortable negotiating on for the right fit.
