## What they do

SMB lending fintech. Fast-decision, flexible loans for small businesses — the kind of credit that a high-street bank takes three months to approve and iwoca turns around in hours. ~250 ppl, profitable, mature Series D-ish stage. The SMB Lending PM owns underwriting product and pricing for the SMB segment: decision models, credit limits, pricing tiers, and the risk-return balance across the portfolio.

## Why it's a fit (or wasn't)

Honest read: iwoca is 250 ppl, above my preferred 80-150 ceiling. That is why it is P1. The credit mechanics are genuinely interesting — SMB pricing has similar messy-middle dynamics to consumer credit (weak signals, thin data, adverse selection pressure), and the lending product is the core business not a feature bolt-on.

I applied 26 days ago and have had no response. That gap on a P1 with a size concern is a weak signal. The SMB-versus-consumer-credit positioning might not have translated clearly enough in the cover note, or the pipeline is slow.

If there is no movement by the next weekly checkpoint, I will close it. Not worth chasing hard when Lendable and Marshmallow are ahead of it on every dimension.
