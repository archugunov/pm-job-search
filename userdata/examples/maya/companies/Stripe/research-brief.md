# Research Brief — Stripe

## What they do

Stripe is a payments infrastructure platform: APIs that let businesses accept money, manage subscriptions, and handle payouts. The Lead PM, Growth role I applied for sat inside the merchant-facing side of the business — specifically activation funnels that move newly signed-up merchants from account creation to first successful transaction. The core metric is time-to-first-charge and the levers are onboarding UX, documentation quality, and integration support.

## Why it's a fit (or wasn't)

Honest read: the rubric said P1 because Stripe is a top-tier fintech brand and the role is senior enough. The domain fit was shakier than the score suggested. My positioning is consumer credit and lending — helping end-users make better borrowing decisions. Stripe Growth optimises for merchant onboarding, which is a B2B activation problem. Adjacent skill set, but the take-home made the gap concrete: I was designing metrics for merchant behaviour, not consumer financial journeys, and the panel could tell the difference.

The rejection note — "going with a candidate with more direct payments experience" — confirmed it rather than surprised me. I'm keeping Stripe on monitoring because a Consumer Credit or Lending product role there would flip the fit entirely. That's the version where my positioning lands rather than requires translation.
