## Why this fits

- **Domain:** Messy-middle, thin-file, post-initial-credit users — exactly the segment Plaid Consumer Credit is targeting and exactly my positioning.
- **Skills carry over:** Bureau-integration work and the pricing-experiment programme map directly to onboarding + risk-pricing trade-offs at the team's stage.
- **Team shape:** 18 ppl but with parent-co compliance, infrastructure, distribution — better leverage than a true Series A.
- **Founder-distance:** Founder-driven bet means reporting close to the CEO, not through five product layers. The autonomy profile I want.
- **Geography:** London, hybrid 2 days from Shoreditch. Clean fit.
- **Honest flag:** Pricing decisions get gated by Capital Markets, not by the CC PM. Could be friction or a non-issue — need to ask Tom directly in the CPO round before accepting any offer.
