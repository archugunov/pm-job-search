# Offer evaluation — Lendable (2026-05-17)

## Verdict

negotiate: comp is mid-band and the role shape is the clearest fit in the pipeline, but the offer arrived 2.5 months ahead of my target date (2026-08-01) — there is no urgency, and first-pass offers at founder-led companies almost always have headroom.

## Comp shape

- Total comp Y1: £155K base + 0.15% equity (unvested, estimated value ~£90K at current £600M implied valuation per Levels.fyi comps) + £20K sign-on = ~£175K Y1 all-in
- Total comp 4Y: £155K × 4 = £620K base + 0.15% equity (diluted ~20% to 0.12% by assumed next funding round) at £2B exit scenario ≈ £2.4M total equity notional; realistically £2.4M × 30% liquidity discount = ~£700K. 4Y total all-in: £620K + £700K + £20K = ~£1.34M
- Base-vs-equity trade-off: equity upside is real but dependent on IPO path materialising within 4-5 years; if IPO slips, the equity is illiquid and the comp story is base-only, which at £155K is mid-range for a London HoP role at this stage

## Role-shape re-check

Build-verdict: this is a build role, not a defend role — the secured credit line is in early build, the consumer credit product is post-PMF-seeking. Archetype match: strong. I've operated in exactly this structure (B2B infrastructure parent, direct-to-consumer product in build, small founder-adjacent team). Anti-goal check: 120 ppl sits inside the 80-150 preferred band; no relocation required; no crypto; Tom is 1 skip away, not 5 layers up. Anti-goals are not triggered.

## Negotiation moves

1. Equity floor: ask for 0.2% (vs offered 0.15%), tied to a specific outcome anchor — for example, reaching portfolio NPL <2% by end of Y2 as a milestone unlocking accelerated vesting on the incremental tranche. Rationale: 0.15% is the bottom of the Levels.fyi range for this stage; 0.2% is the midpoint. Anchoring it to an outcome Tom cares about makes it easier to agree than a flat percentage ask.
2. Sign-on: ask for £30K (vs offered £20K), framed as a relocation buffer and transition cost. Rationale: moving from a current role into a HoP position at a new company typically involves a gap in variable compensation and a notice period cost; £30K is defensible against that framing and doesn't reopen the base conversation.
3. Start date: ask to push to 2026-07-15 (approximately 4 weeks from a nominal June start), framed as wrapping current commitments cleanly. Rationale: a clean handover is in Lendable's interest too — a rushed start risks a difficult first 90 days. This also preserves optionality if other late-stage processes (Plaid CPO round) resolve before then.
