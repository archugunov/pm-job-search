## Company snapshot

- **Stage:** ~2500 ppl, UK digital bank, IPO-track.
- **What they sell:** Consumer banking + lending in-app. Lending is one revenue surface among many, not the company's main bet.
- **The role:** Lead PM on the Lending pod — overdrafts, personal loans, BNPL-style products.

## Why this fits

**P2 — surface fit is exact, org shape is the problem.**

- **Domain:** Overdrafts and short-tenor consumer credit are within one step of my BNPL and thin-file work.
- **Scale upside:** Real lending P&L, large customer base — product judgement is sharp.
- **Size mismatch:** 2500 ppl is well past my preferred band. Role-shape at that scale typically narrows: more platform dependencies, longer roadmap horizons, less direct line to founders.
- **Title inflation risk:** "Lead" at Monzo probably resembles "Senior PM with influence" at a smaller co — strong external signal, day-to-day surface shrinks.
- **Verdict:** Worth a single conversation if Lendable doesn't land. Not a primary target.
