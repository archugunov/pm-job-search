- **Size + stage:** 150 ppl is at the top of my preferred 80-150 band; Series C means recent funding without post-IPO bureaucracy.

- **Positioning fit:** Financial-wellness framing gives the lending surface a clear "outcomes for the borrower" mandate — matches how I think about responsible credit.
