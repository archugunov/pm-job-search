## What they do

UK challenger bank, app-only, ~450 ppl. Based in Durham. The HoP role is multi-team management with 8+ direct reports — a GM job in practice, not an IC-leadership role.

## Why it's NOT a fit

Closed pre-application. Two anti-goal violations make the call easy: 450 ppl is three times my preferred size ceiling, and 8+ direct reports is the GM-shape job I explicitly do not want. I am not looking to manage managers; I want to be close to the product and close to the founding team.

Capturing this for the pattern. At bank-scale shops, even HoP titles tend to resolve into people-management jobs with budget ownership and cross-functional coordination as the primary work. That is not the role I am optimising for. When both the size and the scope fail the anti-goal check, the right move is to close immediately rather than spend time on due diligence.
